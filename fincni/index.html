Need to improve your cyber security?

Energy sector is critical, needs to be protected and security can't be outsourced. We would love to share our experience from infrastructure protection in Finland and hear your feedback on pain points and needs in your specific context.

What we offer

We are three companies from Oulu, so it was easy to share our experiences and to decide on a campaign to cover pain points we have encountered.

Critical network protection

Networks controlling critical processes are often isolated and have static assets and processes. But how does one know that the isolation is leakproof and there are no rogue assets? SensorFu's Beacon tests the vulnerabilities in network isolation on continuous basis and alerts if the isolation fails. SensorFleet's sensor platform keeps track of assets in network and alerts if new actors are discovered.

Office protection

Attacks against soft targets work, as criminals leverage third-party data breaches, employee account takeovers and personal devices in the first phase of the attack. Badrap's service turns your employees into your first layers of defence, stopping attacks where they happen. SensorFleet's sensor platform is your second layer. It provides a cost-effective way to detect and investigate compromises at your office locations.
