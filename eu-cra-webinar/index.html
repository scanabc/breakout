EU Cyber Resilience Act:
The impact on OT operators

Everybody's talking about NIS2 — but how about the new Cyber Resilience Act? In this presentation, Senior OT Security Advisor Mats Karlsson Landré from AFRY will decode how does the EU CRA affect OT operators.

Join this expert webinar on how to steer your way in the changing regulation over cyber security. We all have been introduced to the NIS2 directive, but what is the EU Cyber Resilience Act and how does it affect OT operators? Our webinar focuses on the impact of new regulation to OT operators and their environments.

Our guest speaker is Mats Karlsson Landré, Senior OT Security Advisor with AFRY. Mats Karlsson Landré has over 30 years of experience of cyber security in industrial settings and the nuclear sector. He is also known in the Swedish-speaking world as the editor of the OT-Säkerher newsletter.
